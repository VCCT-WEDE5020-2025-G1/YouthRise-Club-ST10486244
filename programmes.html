Our Programmes

Creative Programme

The Creative Programme encourages youth to explore their artistic talents through workshops in painting, drama, music, creative writing, and dance. Participants are provided a safe space to express themselves, develop confidence, and collaborate with others on projects. By the end of the programme, students can showcase their creative work in exhibitions, performances, or online platforms.

Career Programme

The Career Programme is designed to prepare youth for the professional world. It includes mentorship from experienced professionals, CV and cover letter workshops, interview practice sessions, job shadowing opportunities, and entrepreneurship guidance. Participants gain practical skills, career insights, and networking opportunities to help them make informed choices about their future.

Leadership Programme

The Leadership Programme focuses on building essential leadership and teamwork skills. Through workshops on communication, problem-solving, decision-making, and project management, participants develop confidence and learn to lead initiatives effectively. Mentoring from community leaders helps students apply their skills to real-life projects and become positive role models in their communities.

Additional Programme

Our Additional Programme offers opportunities for youth to engage in community service, volunteering projects, and team-building activities. It provides a platform for personal growth, social responsibility, and connecting with peers to make a meaningful impact in their communities.

All YouthRise Club programmes are designed to empower young people by developing creativity, career readiness, and leadership skills. Participants gain confidence, practical knowledge, and opportunities to engage with their community, build meaningful relationships, and showcase their talents in various platforms.
