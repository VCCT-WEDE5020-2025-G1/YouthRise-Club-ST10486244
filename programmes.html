YouthRise Club Programmes

Creative Programme

The Creative Programme encourages youth to explore their artistic talents through workshops in painting, drama, music, creative writing, and dance. Participants develop confidence and can showcase their work in exhibitions or performances.

Career Programme

The Career Programme prepares youth for the professional world with mentorship, CV workshops, interview practice, job shadowing, and entrepreneurship guidance. Participants gain practical skills and networking opportunities.

Leadership Programme

The Leadership Programme builds essential communication, teamwork, and decision-making skills. Participants are mentored by community leaders and apply their skills to real-life projects to become positive role models.

Additional Programme

The Additional Programme offers community service, volunteering, and team-building activities, helping youth grow personally, socially, and engage meaningfully with their communities.

All YouthRise Club programmes are designed to empower young people by developing creativity, career readiness, and leadership skills. Participants gain confidence, practical knowledge, and opportunities to connect with their community and showcase their talents.
