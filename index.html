Founded in 2018 in Durban, YouthRise Club is a non-profit organisation dedicated to empowering young people through leadership training, career mentorship, and creative expression workshops.

Our mission is to inspire and uplift youth through skills, mentorship, and community-driven programmes.

We envision a generation of confident, skilled youth shaping the future of South Africa.
